Artificial Intelligence is wide-ranging branch of computer science which deals with building smart machines have the ability to perform tasks which require human intelligence.
Artificial intelligence is an interdisciplinary science with multiple approaches,
but advantages in machine learning and deep learning are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the technology industry.
AI has two categories:
1) Narrow AI (weak AI):this kind of artificial intelligence is focused on performing a single task.
These mashines may seem intelligent but they are operating under far more constrains and limitations than even the most basic human intelligence.
2) General AI (Strong AI): we can see this type in the movies like robots, and it can apply the intelligence to solve any problem.
